Privacy Policy

SMS Messaging Privacy Policy

By providing your mobile number to My Medical Centers, you consent to receive SMS messages from us regarding appointment reminders, health tips, clinic updates, and other relevant information.

Consent to Receive Messages

By opting in, you acknowledge and agree that you are providing express written consent to receive SMS messages from My Medical Centers. These messages may be sent via automated systems.

No Third-Party Marketing

Your personal information, including your phone number, will never be shared or sold to third parties for marketing purposes. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your data secure.

Message Frequency

Messages may be recurring and vary in frequency depending on your interactions with us (e.g., appointments, ongoing care). Typically, you may receive between 2 to 6 messages per month.

Opt-Out Instructions

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any of our messages. You will receive a confirmation message and will no longer receive text communications from us.

Help Instructions

For assistance or more information, you can reply HELP at any time. You may also contact our support team directly at patient@mymedicalcenters.ca .

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Please check with your provider for details.

AI Usage at My Medical Centers

My Medical Centers uses artificial intelligence (AI) tools to assist our healthcare professionals with tasks such as medical charting, document preparation, and workflow optimization. These AI systems help doctors save time, reduce administrative burden, and improve the accuracy and efficiency of patient care documentation. All AI-assisted processes are overseen by licensed medical staff, and patient privacy and data security remain our highest priority.
